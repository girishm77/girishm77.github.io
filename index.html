Girish Mallapragada

My research examines how innovation, social media, networks, and inter-firm relationships shape marketing strategy and firm performance.

My work brings digital-trace data, network analysis, and modern econometrics to substantive questions in marketing strategy — from open-source and crowdfunding communities to political communication, salesperson networks, and firm governance. It has appeared in the Journal of Marketing, Marketing Science, Journal of Marketing Research, Management Science, and the Journal of the Academy of Marketing Science.

Research

I study innovation, social media, marketing channels, and marketing strategy — often using network structure, digital-trace data, and text as a window into how firms and communities create and capture value.
